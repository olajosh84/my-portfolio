about me

Hi, my name is jekiayinfa olumuyiwa and I am a web developer. I started my development journey back in August 2014 as a back-end web developer using Php and Mysql. However, my coding days came to an abrupt end a year and a half later because I ventured into other things. During the aforementioned period, I participated in building of a number of web applications which included: A Medical Application, an Excel Upload Application, an Inventory Management Application among others.

In 2021, I decided to come back by building a Business Directory Application(although it is now offline) and in the end I realised I needed to expand my knowledge to include other languages bordering on front-end and back-end programming. And so this informed my decision, in 2022, to learn Javascript, React JS, Node JS and other languages. I must say I have made trmendous progress so far because I have been able to, with the little knowledge I have gathered so far, build a few applications some of which are included in my portfolio.

Some of my biggest strengths include: Teaming up with others to achieve a goal because I believe in team-spirit; I pay very close attention to details; I always put myself in the shoes of the user when I am developing any application; I am a man of my word; I am very honest and expect everyone to be honest with me, especially if I am making a mistake.
